What is an Electric Instant Water heater?

Electric water heaters operate on electricity, consuming electrical energy to heat the water. This type of water heater is predominantly used in HDB flats because of its compact size. Again Electric instant water is classified into two types.

1. Single Point Electric Instant Water Heater

Electric instant water heaters pass water through a single point and to the outlet.

2. Multi Point Electric Instant Water Heater

Multi-point Electric Instant Water Heater passes water through multiple points and to the outlet.

What is a Digital Instant Water Heater?

Digital Instant Water Heaters are primarily used in Condominiums because it's a bit more expensive than electric instant water heaters. With this type of water heater, you can set the temperature according to your wish.

What is a Gas Instant Water Heater?

Gas Instant Water Heaters operate with gas to heat the water. Usually, this type of water heater is used in Industries and less frequently in residential areas because this type of heater can deliver hot water very quickly compared to other kinds of water heaters.

Factors to consider before buying a water heater?

Factors to consider before buying a water heater?
1. Usage

If the water heater is required for occasional use and only 1 or 2 member usage, then go for it.

2. Consistent Hot Water

When choosing your water heater, ensure it maintains a consistent temperature. So there is no drop in hot water temperature or super high temperature all of a sudden.

3. Cost/Budget

If you are on a tight budget, the best option is to go for an Instant water heater, as they are very affordable.

4. Energy Saving

Instant water heaters consume less significantly energy.

5. Space Requirements

Instant water heaters occupy relatively lesser spaces when compared with storage water heaters, so if you have a small bathroom, choose this.
